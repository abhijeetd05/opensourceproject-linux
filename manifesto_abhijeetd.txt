===== Open Source Manifesto =====
Author: abhijeetd
Date: 30 March 2026

I believe in the power of open source.
Every day, I rely on python, a tool that represents collaboration and innovation.
To me, freedom means Free — the ability to learn, modify, and share without limits.
I aspire to build food and share it openly with the world,
so that others can benefit, improve it, and continue the cycle of knowledge.

Open source is not just code; it is a movement.
